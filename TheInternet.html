The Internet


What is it?:

The Internet is a network in which computers share and exchange information. It is built up of many necessary layers that each preform a significant role.

The Layers:

HTTP: Manages sending and receiving files
RRandP: Messages split into packets
DNS: Translates domains into IP addresses
